About me

Hi, I'm Yelitza Choque, a designer in love with mobile web, minimalism, and UX.

I like agile methodologies as Design Thinking or Design Sprint and I try to work with them. But, when I can't, I focus myself to ask the reason of things and create prototypes in post-it.

When I'm not prototyping, I invest my time in learning new technologies and tools on my own. You can find me always with my headphones on my head or a book in my hand.
